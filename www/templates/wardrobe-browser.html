On Mobile Chrome

1. Tap address bar from mobile browser. 2. Tap the    symbol from mobile browser. 4. You should see SnapLook in your list of apps. Now, you can easily save items to SnapLook while you're browsing.

On Other Browsers

1. Tap the    symbol from mobile browser.

2. You should see SnapLook in your list of apps. Now, you can easily save items to SnapLook while you're browsing.

On iOS 13

1. Tap the    symbol from mobile browser. 2. Scroll to the right to find the "More" button and tap it. 3. Tap edit at the top right. 4. Find SnapLook in your list of apps and make sure it's toggled "on". 5. Tap the "+" on the left side of the app to add it to the share menu. 6. If you want to change the order of apps that appear in your share menu, tap on hold the bars to the far right to move SnapLook closer to the top. 7. Tap "Done" and you should see SnapLook in your list of apps. Now, you can easily save items to SnapLook while you're browsing.

On iOS 12 and earlier

1. Tap the    symbol from mobile browser. 2. Scroll to the right to find the "More" button. 3. Find SnapLook in your list of apps and toggle it on. Pro tip: Use the bars next to the toggle button to slide apps up and down and put them in your preferred order. We suggest putting SnapLook at the top! 4. Tap "Done" and you should see SnapLook in your list of apps. Now, you can easily save items to SnapLook while you're browsing.
